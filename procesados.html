Avante Gourmet SA de CV

“Avante” which in Latin means advance, was founded in 1950 by Nicolás Paul Rauda, ​​who started this company from Córdoba making chipotle peppers and sauces in his own home. Introducing the now traditional Avante products among his friends and inside the "Paul" grocery store.
The brand was consolidated for several years as a typical product of Córdoba and with great success throughout the State of Veracruz, being recognized as the most emblematic variety of sauces and chili peppers of great flavor and quality in the central zone of Veracruz territory.
